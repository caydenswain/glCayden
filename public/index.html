About Us

GuardianLink empowers non-profits with essential cybersecurity services to safeguard their data and resources in a digital world where security is a must, not just for businesses but for vulnerable non-profits operating on tight budgets; by providing access to top-tier cybersecurity expertise and tools, GuardianLink enhances their security, making these organizations less attractive to cybercriminals and helping them focus on their mission. Additionally, GuardianLink invites cybersecurity professionals and volunteers to join and contribute to a safer digital future for non-profits, emphasizing collaboration and active participation in bolstering cybersecurity defenses. GuardianLink aims to strengthen cybersecurity defenses of non-profit organizations by connecting them with skilled cybersecurity volunteers, providing robust, reliable, and accessible support to those making the world a better place. Founded in 2021 by a group of cybersecurity experts and philanthropists, it started as a small initiative to help local charities protect themselves online, and has since expanded across the country, enhancing its service offerings and volunteer network.

Our Services

At GuardianLink, we specialize in tailored cybersecurity solutions for non-profits, addressing unique challenges to safeguard digital assets and sensitive information. Our services include vulnerability assessments, incident response, and continuous monitoring, supported by education and training sessions to equip staff with necessary skills, ensuring security and preparedness against cyber threats. We also provide custom security solutions to meet specific organizational needs.
